# Pre-Submit Gate

## Claims

- Core claim is singular and obvious.
- Every major claim maps to evidence.
- Scope matches actual evidence.

## Evaluation

- Mandatory baselines are present.
- Key ablations are present.
- No suspicious cherry-picked setup dominates the story.

## Writing

- Abstract and introduction do not overclaim.
- Figures and tables are interpretable on first read.
- Limitations are visible.

## Decision

- Submit
- Submit with eyes open
- Delay
- Retarget
